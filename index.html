Merfolk population is rapidly declining due to Global warming upsetting the balance of the ocean's eco-systems! Our organization, Save the Merfolk (STM), strives to not only provide emergency assistance to stranded and injured merfolk but raise awareness to maintain and help the current merpeople population recover.

The site of our facility is on Fernandina Island of the Galapagos Islands. STM is fast approaching our grand opening of our new Rehabilitation Center that not only will provide a headquarters for our rescue efforts but will provide housing for our injured Merfolk and a state-of-the-art birthing center.
