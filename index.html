Merpeople population is rapidly declining due Global warming upsetting the balance of the oceans eco-system! Our organization strives to not only provide emergency assistance to stranded and injured mermaids but raise awareness to maintain and help the current Merpeople population recover.

The site of our facility is on Fernandina Island of the Galapagos Islands. Our new state of the art Merpeople research and rehab facility is now up and running.
